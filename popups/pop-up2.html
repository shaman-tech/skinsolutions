Dr. Kingsley R. Chin, Spine Surgeon

Professor and Harvard-trained surgeon Dr. Kingsley R. Chin has taught over 200 surgeons from several countries and has provided his expertise to children, professional athletes, and patients around the world. He is the founder of the LESS Institute, and has devoted many years to laying the foundation for less invasive surgery techniques. The LESS Institute was founded as a center of excellence to advance the industry standard of patient care.
