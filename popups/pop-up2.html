Dr. Norman Morrison Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Morrison is an acclaimed and highly sought after Plastic and Reconstructive surgeon in the greater New York City area and Jamaica. Having been in private practice for over 20 years, Dr. Morrison has helped hundreds of patients transform their lives through surgical enhancements. He is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, the American Board of Surgery and by the Fellows of the American College of Surgeons. With a focus in patient care and getting results, Dr. Morrison is hands-on in guiding each patient through their surgical journey and ensuring individual satisfaction with their results. Dr. Morrison received his Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) from University of the West Indies Faculty of Medicine in 1980, and later moved to New York City to continue his surgical training. He later served as a resident in General Surgery at North General Hospital and completed a Plastic Surgery Fellowship at Harlem Hospital in before founding Morrison Plastic Surgery Practice in 1994 in New York City.
