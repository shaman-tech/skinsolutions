Dr. Stephanie Lyew, General Surgeon Spine Surgeon

Dr. Stephanie Lyew is a specialist in arterial and venous diseases, hernia repair and major gastrointestinal procedures. During her residency, she completed a 6-month elective at St. Michael's Hospital in Toronto, Canada. There, she garnered experience in breast and colorectal surgery. Dr. Lyew also has a special interest in Breast Oncology. She is a graduate of the General Surgery Programme at UHWI, Campion College and Holy Childhood High schools. Dr. Lyew currently practices as Surgical Consultant at Annotto Bay Hospital and Skin Solutions Ambulatory Surgical Centre.
